## The Lost Monster Difference

**Headline:** Framework-Driven Development (Not Developer-Dependent)

Most agencies rely on individual talent. We rely on proven systems. Here's what that means for you:

### 1. We Plan Before We Code (CODA Framework)

**The Problem:** Most developers jump straight into building. Then change direction halfway through. Then realize they missed key requirements.

**Our Approach:**
- **Context:** We understand your business, your customers, your market position
- **Objective:** We define crystal-clear success metrics (not vague "looks good")
- **Details:** We document every requirement before writing a single line of code
- **Acceptance:** We agree on exactly what "done" looks like before we start

**What You Get:** No surprises. No scope creep. No "oh, we thought you meant..." Every project starts with absolute clarity.

**Time Saved:** 3-5 hours of rework per project (we get it right the first time)

---

### 2. You See 5 Options, Not 1 Guess (Design Variations)

**The Problem:** Most designers show you one option. If you don't like it, they get defensive or start from scratch.

**Our Approach:**
- Every design brief gets **5 distinct variations**
- Different approaches to the same problem
- Explores the design space systematically
- You pick what resonates, we refine it

**What You Get:**
- Real choice (not "do you like this?")
- Confidence you're seeing the best approach (not just the first idea)
- Faster decisions (easier to choose between 5 than critique 1)

**Example:** For a luxury lodge booking site, we delivered:
1. **Nordic Minimal:** Clean lines, white space, let photography breathe
2. **Highland Heritage:** Rich textures, warm tones, traditional luxury
3. **Adventure Bold:** Dynamic layouts, action photography, excitement
4. **Scandi-Scot Fusion:** Nordic minimalism meets Highland warmth (winner)
5. **Modern Classic:** Timeless elegance, subtle luxury cues

Client chose #4. We refined it. Project stayed on track.

---

### 3. Nothing Reaches You Below 80/100 (Quality Checklist)

**The Problem:** Quality varies wildly. Sometimes great, sometimes "we'll fix it in the next sprint."

**Our Approach:**
- Every deliverable goes through our **Pre-Design Quality Checklist**
- Minimum score: 80/100
- Below 80? It doesn't leave our office
- You never see work-in-progress quality

**What We Check:**
- ✅ All requirements met (scope complete)
- ✅ Performance targets hit (load time, responsiveness)
- ✅ Accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1 AA minimum)
- ✅ Mobile-first design (60%+ of traffic)
- ✅ SEO foundations (semantic HTML, meta tags)
- ✅ Browser compatibility (last 2 versions)
- ✅ Code quality (linted, tested, documented)
- ✅ Business goals addressed (conversion optimization)

**What You Get:** Consistent quality. Every time. No exceptions.

---

### 4. We Understand Your Business (Domain Knowledge)

**The Problem:** Most developers treat every project like a code puzzle. They don't understand your market, your customers, or your business model.

**Our Approach:**
- We capture **Domain Knowledge** for every project
- Your target audience (demographics, psychographics, pain points)
- Your value proposition (what makes you different)
- Your industry dynamics (seasonal trends, competitive landscape)
- Your business goals (not just project requirements)

**Why This Matters:**
When AI understands your business context, content quality improves 10x:
- Headlines that resonate (not generic marketing speak)
- Messaging that converts (not features, benefits)
- Design decisions that support business goals

**Example:** For Ancarraig (luxury lodges):
- **Context captured:** Competing with OTAs, targeting premium travelers, 70% bookings going through Booking.com (20% commission)
- **Business goal:** Increase direct bookings to 70%
- **Design decision:** Seamless booking flow (4 clicks to reserve), trust signals (testimonials, awards), urgency triggers (limited availability)
- **Result:** 3-5% conversion rate (vs 2% industry average), 70% direct bookings achieved

We didn't just build a pretty website. We built a booking engine.

## How We Work (Process Overview)

**Headline:** Six Steps From Brief to Launch

### 1. Discovery (Week 1-2)
**What Happens:**
- Deep-dive into your business (not just the project)
- Stakeholder interviews (decision-makers, end users)
- Competitive analysis (what's working in your industry)
- Technical audit (existing systems, integrations needed)

**What You Get:** CODA planning document (Context-Objective-Details-Acceptance)

---

### 2. Strategy (Week 2-3)
**What Happens:**
- Information architecture (site structure, user flows)
- Feature prioritization (MVP vs nice-to-have)
- Technical architecture (tech stack decisions, integrations)
- Project timeline and milestones

**What You Get:** Detailed project roadmap with clear milestones

---

### 3. Design (Week 3-6)
**What Happens:**
- Wireframes (structure and flow)
- 5 design variations (different creative directions)
- You choose, we refine
- High-fidelity mockups (pixel-perfect designs)

**What You Get:** Complete design system + all page templates

---

### 4. Development (Week 6-12)
**What Happens:**
- Component-driven development (reusable, maintainable)
- Weekly progress demos (see it working, not just screenshots)
- Your feedback incorporated continuously
- Quality checks at every stage (80/100 minimum)

**What You Get:** Staging site for testing + feedback

---

### 5. Testing (Week 11-12)
**What Happens:**
- Cross-browser testing (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- Mobile device testing (iOS, Android, multiple screen sizes)
- Performance optimization (load time, Core Web Vitals)
- Accessibility audit (WCAG 2.1 AA compliance)
- Your team UAT (user acceptance testing)

**What You Get:** Bug-free, production-ready system

---

### 6. Launch & Support (Week 12+)
**What Happens:**
- Deployment to production
- DNS configuration, SSL setup
- 30-day post-launch support (bug fixes, adjustments)
- Training for your team (CMS, basic maintenance)

**What You Get:** Live site + documentation + peace of mind
