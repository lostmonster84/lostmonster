**In Practice:**
- Minimum 80/100 on every deliverable

**Trade-off:** We're not the fastest agency. We're the most reliable.

### 2. Systems Over Heroics
We don't rely on rockstar developers pulling all-nighters. We rely on proven frameworks that work every time.

**In Practice:**
- CODA planning (3-5 hours upfront saves 10+ hours in rework)

- Quality Checklists (catch issues before they reach you)

### 3. Business Results Over Technical Elegance
Beautiful code matters. But conversion rates matter more.

**In Practice:**

**Trade-off:** We might not use the shiniest new framework. We use what works.

**In Practice:**
