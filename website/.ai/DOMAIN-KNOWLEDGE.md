## Business Model

- Knowledge capture systems (Domain Knowledge templates)

## Notes for Design Decisions

### Color Palette Considerations
- **Professional:** Not too playful or casual
- **Premium:** High-quality feel, not cheap
- **Trustworthy:** Colors that build confidence
- **Accessible:** WCAG 2.1 AA contrast ratios

### Typography Considerations
- **Clear:** Easy to read, not decorative
- **Hierarchical:** Clear information architecture
- **Professional:** Not trendy or experimental
- **Accessible:** Readable sizes, good line height

### Layout Considerations
- **Structured:** Clear organization, not chaotic
- **Systematic:** Consistent patterns
- **Conversion-focused:** Clear CTAs, trust signals
- **Mobile-first:** 60%+ mobile traffic

### Component Considerations
- **Consistent:** Reusable patterns
- **Accessible:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
- **Performance:** Fast loading, optimized
- **Professional:** Polished appearance
